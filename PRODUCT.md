# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Temu cross-border operators who import Yunqi and 1688 product data, choose sourcing SKUs, manage listing images, and export sheets for manual upload or downstream automation.

## Product Purpose

The workbench centralizes product data, sourcing materials, SKU selection, image generation state, and Excel export so operators can move from collected products to upload-ready listings with fewer manual handoffs.

## Brand Personality

Practical, precise, calm. The interface should feel like an operations console built for repeated daily work, not a marketing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid oversized landing-page sections, decorative gradients, busy image walls, nested cards, and controls that obscure the current task.

## Design Principles

- Keep workflow state visible without overwhelming the operator.
- Prefer compact tables, tabs, and staged details over image-heavy dashboards.
- Make generated outputs traceable back to their source product, selected image, mode, and slot.
- Preserve familiar Ant Design interaction patterns unless the workflow clearly needs a custom surface.
- Keep destructive actions small, explicit, and close to the item they affect.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, keyboard-reachable controls, stable focus states, and reduced visual noise. Motion should communicate state changes only.
